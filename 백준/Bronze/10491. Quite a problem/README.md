### 문제 설명

<p>It gets tiring, looking for all ways in which the word &lsquo;problem&rsquo; can be used (and mis-used) in the news media. And yet, that&rsquo;s been your job for several years: looking through news stories for that word. Wouldn&rsquo;t it be better if you could automate the process?</p>

### 입력

<p>Each line of input is one test case. Lines are at most 80 characters long. There are at most 1000 lines of input. Input ends at end of file.</p>

### 출력

<p>For each line of input, print yes if the line contains &lsquo;problem&rsquo;, and no otherwise. Any capitalization of &lsquo;problem&rsquo; counts as an occurrence.</p>
