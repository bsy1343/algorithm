### 문제 설명

<p>It&rsquo;s getting close to March Madness time and the big game this week is the classic rivalry between State U and U State. The coach of State U has been working night and day on the best strategy to beat his arch rival. On defense he has decided to have his tallest player guard the opponent&rsquo;s tallest player, his second tallest player guard the opponent&rsquo;s second tallest player, and so on. He&rsquo;s a bit worried about the size differences between the two teams though, and needs to know for this defensive strategy how many of his players are taller than the person they&rsquo;re guarding. He&rsquo;s asked you to take time from writing term papers for his players to write a program to calculate this value for him.</p>

### 입력

<p>Input consists of two lines. The first line contains five positive integers specifying the heights of the five players on State U&rsquo;s team (in no particular order). The second line contains the similar information for the five players on U State&rsquo;s team (again in no particular order).</p>

### 출력

<p>Output the number of State U players taller than the person they are guarding assuming that the tallest State U player guards the tallest U State player, the second tallest State U player guards the second tallest U State player, and so on.</p>
