### 문제 설명

<p>A charity is having a silent auction where people place bids on a prize without knowing anyone else&rsquo;s bid. Each bid includes a person&rsquo;s name and the amount of their bid. After the silent auction is over, the winner is the person who has placed the highest bid. If there is a tie, the person whose bid was placed first wins. Your job is to determine the winner of the silent auction.</p>

### 입력

<p>The first line of input contains a positive integer N, where 1 &le; N &le; 100, representing the number of bids collected at the silent auction. Each of the next N pairs of lines contains a person&rsquo;s name on one line, and the amount of their bid, in dollars, on the next line. Each bid is a positive integer less than 2000. The order of the input is the order in which bids were placed.</p>

### 출력

<p>Output the name of the person who has won the silent auction.</p>
