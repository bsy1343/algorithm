### 문제 설명

<p>Governor Weatherby Swann orders you to count the number of coins in the government treasury. To make the job more interesting you decide to say &rdquo;Dead&rdquo; for all numbers that are a multiple of 3 and &rdquo;Man&rdquo; for all numbers that are a multiple of 5. For numbers that are multiples of both 3 and 5 you say &rdquo;DeadMan&rdquo;.</p>

<p> </p>

### 입력

<p>The input will be the number of coins you need to count.</p>

### 출력

<p>The output will be the numbers and words in sequence, separated by spaces. Start a new line after each word.</p>
