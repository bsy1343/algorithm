### 문제 설명

<p>Write a program to print:</p>

<ol>
	<li>The perfect numbers between $1\,000$ and $9\,999$ inclusive. A perfect number is a positive integer which is equal to the sum of its proper divisors. A proper divisor is any divisor less than the number itself. For example, $6$ is a perfect number since $1 + 2 + 3 = 6$.</li>
	<li>All integers between $100$ and $999$ inclusive which are equal to the sum of the cubes of their digits.</li>
</ol>

### 입력

<p>There is no input.</p>

### 출력

<p>All the perfect numbers between $1\,000$ and $9\,999$ inclusive on one line, followed by all integers between $100$ and $999$ inclusive which are equal to the sum of the cubes of their digits on one line.</p>
