### 문제 설명

<p>Christmas comes sooner every year. In fact, in one oft-forgotten corner of the world, gift-giving&nbsp;has already started in the form of a Secret Santa syndicate.</p>

<p>Everybody in the small town of Haircombe is going to put their name into a hat. This hat will&nbsp;be given a hearty shuffle, and then afterwards everybody will take turns once more in taking a&nbsp;name back from the hat.</p>

<p>The name each person receives is the name of the fellow citizen to whom they will send a gift.</p>

<p>Of course, one concern with this strategy is that some unfortunate citizens could wind up&nbsp;giving gifts to themselves. What are the chances that this will happen to any of the citizens of&nbsp;Haircombe?</p>

### 입력

<ul>
	<li>One line containing the number N (1 &le; N &le; 10<sup>12</sup>), the number of citizens who will take&nbsp;part in Secret Santa.</li>
</ul>

### 출력

<ul>
	<li>One line containing one real number; the probability that one or more people wind up&nbsp;giving gifts to themselves.</li>
</ul>

<p>All output must be accurate to an absolute or relative error of at most 10<sup>&minus;6</sup>.</p>
