### 문제 설명

<p>Bobo has three points, namely, point $A$, $B$ and $C$. And now he wants to find a point $P$ to minimize $|PA| + 2 \cdot |PB| + 3 \cdot |PC|$.</p>

<p>Note that $|AB|$ denotes the Euclidian distance between points $A$ and $B$.</p>

### 입력

<p>Each of the $3$ lines contains $2$ integers $x_i, y_i$, which denotes the coordinates of point $A, B, C$, respectively ($|x_i|, |y_i| \leq 10000$).</p>

### 출력

<p>A single float number denotes the minimum of total distance. Absolutely or relatively error within $10^{-6}$ will get accepted.</p>
