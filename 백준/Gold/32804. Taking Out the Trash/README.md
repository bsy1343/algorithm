### 문제 설명

<p>Peter has way too much trash and he needs to take it all out.</p>

<p>Specifically, there are $n$ bags of trash each with a specific weight. Peter can hold either one or two bags of trash per trip, and he can carry a maximum total of $m$ milligrams of trash in a single trip. What is the minimum number of trips Peter needs to take to take out all the trash?</p>

### 입력

<p>The input starts with two integers $n$ $(1 \le n \le 5 \cdot 10^5)$ and $m$ $(1 \le m \le 10^9)$, the number of bags of trash and the maximum weight of trash Peter can carry.</p>

<p>The next line contains $n$ integers, $w_i$ $(1 \le w_i \le m)$, the weight of each bag of trash in milligrams.</p>

### 출력

<p>Output the minimum number of trips Peter needs to make to take out all the trash.</p>
