<p>Your task is to develop a strategy that guarantees you win at Rock, Paper, Scissors every time. Yes, you heard that right --- and there's no trickery involved!</p>

<p>Now, let me quickly recap the rules for those who might not be familiar. In this game, two players each select one of three symbols: rock, paper, or scissors. <p>In this contest, you will face off in three separate games, with each game consisting of $20$ rounds. To emerge victorious in a game, you must win more rounds than you lose. Your ultimate goal is to win at least two out of the three games.</p>
