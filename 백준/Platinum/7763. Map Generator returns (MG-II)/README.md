### 문제 설명

<p>You have just finished your research of FAMI algorithm (see task &ldquo;Map Generator&rdquo;). You are very proud of yourself and even expect being paid bonus this&nbsp;month. Your boss, whose nickname is Dean, asks you to come over to his office for a moment. You expect thanks and even promotion. But &hellip;</p>

<p>&lsquo;What is this?&rsquo; asks Dean and shows you your latest report.</p>

<p>&lsquo;Well, h-m-m, these are results of my FAMI algorithm research, a-a-a,&rsquo; you answer. It seems that your dreams about soon promotion are &hellip; just dreams. But you still do not understand what is wrong about your report.</p>

<p>&lsquo;I can read, by the way,&rsquo; continues Dean. &lsquo;I am talking about the absolute error. Why it is so big? I require better results!&rsquo;</p>

<p>When you argue with your boss, the best argument is silence. So, now instead of bonuses and promotions you need to rewrite your program.</p>

### 입력

<p>The input, as in the previous task, consists of two lines. The first line contains an integer number N (1 &le; N &le; 20), while the second line contains a real number P (0 &le; P &le; 1).&nbsp;</p>

### 출력

<p>The output needs to contain an answer - the single real number which is the probability of generating connected map by FAMI algorithm. Absolute error of the answer must not exceed 10<sup>-8</sup>.</p>
