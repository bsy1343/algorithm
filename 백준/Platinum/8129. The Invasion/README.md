### 문제 설명

<p>And so it has come - the Triangles have invaded Byteotia! Byteotia lies on an island, occupying its entire surface. The shape of the island is a convex polygon (i.e. a polygon whose each inner angle is smaller than 180&deg;). A certain number of software factories are located in Byteotia, each of which generates constant gains or losses.</p>

<p>The Triangles have decided to occupy such a part of Byteotia which:</p>

<ul>
	<li>is a triangle-shaped area, the vertices of which are three different vertices of the polygon-island,</li>
	<li>brings the largest income i.e. the sum of all gains and losses generated by factories within the occupied area is maximal.</li>
</ul>

<p>We assume that a factory located on the border or in the vertex of occupied area belongs to that area. A territory which contains no factory brings, obviously, a zero income.</p>

<p>Byteasar, the King of Byteotia, is concerned by the amount of losses the Triangles&#39; invasion could generate. Help him by writing a programme which shall calculate the sum of gains and losses generated by factories which the Triangles wish to capture.</p>

<p>Write a programme which:</p>

<ul>
	<li>reads a decription of Byteotia&#39;s shape and the locations of factories from the input file,</li>
	<li>determines the maximal value of sum of all gains and losses generated by factories within a triangle, whose vertices are three different vertices of the polygon island,</li>
	<li>writes the outcome to the output file.</li>
</ul>

### 입력

<p>The first line of the input file contains a single integer n (3 &le; n &le; 600), denoting the number of vertices of the polygon-island. The following n lines of the input contain two integers each x<sub>i</sub> and y<sub>i</sub> (-10,000 &le; x<sub>i</sub>,y<sub>i</sub> &le; 10,000), separated by a single space, denoting the coordinates x and y of consecutive vertices of the island, in a clockwise order. The n+2-nd line contains a single integer m (1 &le; m &le; 10,000), denoting the total number of factories. In each of the following m lines there are three integers x&rsquo;<sub>i</sub>, y&rsquo;<sub>i</sub> and w<sub>i</sub> (-10,000 &le; x&rsquo;<sub>i</sub>,y&rsquo;<sub>i</sub> &le; 10,000, -100,000 &le; w<sub>i</sub> &le; 100,000), separated by single spaces, denoting: the coordinates x and y of the i-th factory and the gain (for w<sub>i</sub> &ge; 0) or loss (for w<sub>i</sub> &lt; 0) this factory generates, respectively. Each factory is situated on the polygon-island i.e. within or on the border of it. Distinct factories may be located in the same place i.e. have the same coordinates.</p>

### 출력

<p>The first and only line of the output file should contain a single integer denoting the maximal value of sum of all gains and losses generated by factories within a triangle whose vertices are three different vertices of the polygon-island. Notice that it may happen that the outcome is a negative integer.</p>
