### 문제 설명

<p>Russian scientists are working on several promising topics. One of them is the quality of laugh. In this research they analyse the human speech and extract the laugh from it.</p>

<p>The scientists already made the software which converts speech to text. They consider a string of alternating letters &ldquo;<code>h</code>&rdquo; and &ldquo;<code>a</code>&rdquo; to be a laugh. For example, strings &ldquo;<code>ahahaha</code>&rdquo;, &ldquo;<code>hah</code>&rdquo; and &ldquo;<code>a</code>&rdquo; are laughs, but &ldquo;<code>abacaba</code>&rdquo; and &ldquo;<code>h</code>h&rdquo; are not.</p>

<p>You are given a string&nbsp;<em>s</em> containing the text. Find the length of the longest substring which is considered a laugh.</p>
